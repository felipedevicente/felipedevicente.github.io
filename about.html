About Felipe De Vicente

Felipe De Vicente is a Brazilian visual artist and electronic musician. His work spans abstract digital art, NFTs, and electronic music in genres like house, techno, progressive house, ambient, and more.

Felipe explores the connection between visual and musical compositions, often giving his artworks the same title as his music pieces. He experiments with color, form, and sound to create a unique audiovisual experience.
